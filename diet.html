Foods to Avoid

  • Processed foods (chips, candies)
  • High-sugar beverages (sodas, energy drinks)
  • Refined carbs (white bread, pastries)
  • Trans fats (fried foods)
  • Dairy (can affect hormones)

Foods to Eat

  • High-fiber foods (broccoli, spinach)
  • Healthy fats (avocados, nuts)
  • Lean proteins (chicken, tofu)
  • Anti-inflammatory foods (berries, turmeric)

PCOS Management Tips

  • Exercise regularly (e.g., yoga, walking)
  • Stay hydrated
  • Manage stress through hobbies
  • Eat balanced meals
